- **104 total matches** (up from 64 in 2022)
- **16 host cities** across 3 countries

### Match Statistics
- **Total goals**: 281 from 172 different scorers
- **Average goals per match**: 2.75
- **Hat-tricks**: 2 (Mbappé, Haaland)
