## Group Stage Pairing System

Each group follows a consistent pairing format:

### Matchday 1
- Team 1 vs Team 2
- Team 3 vs Team 4

### Matchday 2
- Team 1 vs Team 3
- Team 2 vs Team 4

### Matchday 3
- Team 4 vs Team 1
- Team 3 vs Team 2

This ensures every team plays 3 matches against different opponents.

---

## Tie-Breaking Criteria

The ranking of teams in each group was determined by:
1. **Points obtained** in all group matches (3 for win, 1 for draw, 0 for loss)
2. **Goal difference** (goals scored minus goals conceded)
3. **Goals scored**
4. **Fair play points** (yellow/red cards)
5. **Drawing of lots** by FIFA Organizing Committee

For third-place team rankings (to determine the 8 best third-placed teams):
- Same criteria applied, with goal difference taking priority
- Teams are ranked across all 12 groups
- The 8 best third-placed teams advance to the Round of 16

### Automatic Qualifiers
45 teams qualified automatically through their respective confederation tournaments:
- **UEFA**: 13 teams (Germany, Spain, France, England, Belgium, Netherlands, Croatia, Portugal, Italy, Denmark, Austria, Serbia, Romania)
- **CONMEBOL**: 6 teams (Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Uruguay, Ecuador, Peru)
- **CONCACAF**: 3 teams (USA, Canada, Mexico) + playoff winners
- **CAF**: 9 teams (Morocco, Senegal, Nigeria, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Tunisia, Cameroon, Algeria, Egypt)
- **AFC**: 8 teams (Japan, South Korea, Iran, Saudi Arabia, Australia, UAE, Iraq, Qatar)
- **OFC**: 1 team (New Zealand)

### Playoff Qualifiers
3 spots were determined through intercontinental playoffs and UEFA Nations League paths.
