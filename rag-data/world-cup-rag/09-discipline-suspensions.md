## Fair Play Points System

### Deductions
| Offense | Deduction |
|---------|-----------|
| Yellow card | -1 point |
| Indirect red card (second yellow) | -3 points |
| Direct red card | -4 points |
| Yellow card + direct red card | -5 points |

**Note**: Only one deduction could be applied to a player or team coach/official in a single match.
