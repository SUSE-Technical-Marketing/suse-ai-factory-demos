### Tiebreaker Criteria for Third-Place Ranking
When teams are tied on points, the following tiebreakers are applied:
1. Goal difference (GF - GA)
2. Goals scored (GF)
3. Fair play points (yellow/red cards)
4. Drawing of lots by FIFA Organizing Committee

### Teams That Did NOT Advance
All 12 third-place teams failed to advance to the knockout stage, as teams finishing 2nd in Groups B, D, and K also had sufficient records.

**Notable eliminations:**
- **Sweden** (Group F): 4 points, 7 goals scored, 0 goal difference
- **Austria** (Group J): 4 points, 6 goals scored, 0 goal difference
- **Bosnia and Herzegovina** (Group B): 4 points, 5 goals scored, -1 goal difference
- **Australia** (Group D): 4 points, 2 goals scored, 0 goal difference
