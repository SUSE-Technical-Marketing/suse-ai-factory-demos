## Venue Highlights

### Estadio Azteca (Mexico City)
- **Capacity**: 80,824
- **Historic**: Only stadium to host TWO World Cup Finals (1970, 1986)
- **2026**: Will host the Final (3rd time hosting a final)
- **Matches**: 5 total

### MetLife Stadium (East Rutherford, NJ)
- **Capacity**: 80,663
- **Significance**: Opening match venue AND Final venue
- **Matches**: 8 total (most in USA)

### SoFi Stadium (Inglewood, CA)
- **Capacity**: 70,492
- **Significance**: Hosts both Semifinals AND Third Place Match
- **Matches**: 8 total
