## Historical Context

### 2026 FIFA World Cup Records
- **First World Cup** hosted by three nations (Canada, Mexico, United States)
- **First World Cup** with 48 teams (expanded from 32)
- **104 total matches** (up from 64 in 2022)
- **16 host cities** across 3 countries

### World Cup Hosting History
- **2026**: Canada, Mexico, United States (Joint) - First trinationals host
- **2022**: Qatar - First Middle Eastern host
- **2018**: Russia - Second European host after 2014
- **2014**: Brazil - First World Cup in Brazil since 1950
- **2010**: South Africa - First African host

### Notable World Cup Firsts in 2026
1. **48-team format**: Expanded from 32 teams starting in 2026
2. **Trinationals hosting**: First time three nations co-host
3. **104 matches**: Record number of matches in a single World Cup
4. **North American hosting**: Third consecutive World Cup in Americas (2014 Brazil, 2022 Qatar... correction: 2026 is first North American World Cup since 1994 USA)

### Stadium Records
- **Estadio Azteca** (Mexico City): Only stadium to host two World Cup Finals (1970, 1986), now hosting the 2026 Final
- **MetLife Stadium** (East Rutherford): Host of the 2026 Final

## Tournament Storylines

### Messi's Legacy
Lionel Messi, already a World Cup champion (2022), continues his quest for additional glory. With 8 goals in the tournament, he is tied for the Golden Boot lead.

### France's Attacking Prowess
France has been the tournament's highest-scoring team with 18 goals, led by Kylian Mbappé's 8 goals. Their attacking trio of Mbappé, Dembélé, and others has been devastating.

### Spain's Defensive Wall
Spain has conceded just 1 goal in 4 matches, the best defensive record in the tournament. Their 7 goals scored and 1 conceded gives them a +6 goal difference.

### Argentina's Consistency
Argentina has been unbeaten throughout the tournament, matching Messi's 2022 dominance. Their 8 goals scored and 3 conceded shows a balanced approach.

### Host Nation Performances
- **United States**: Eliminated in Round of 16 by Belgium (1-4)
- **Canada**: Reached Round of 16 before losing to Morocco (0-3)
- **Mexico**: Eliminated in Round of 16 by England (2-3)

### Notable Upsets
1. **Norway 2-1 Brazil** (Round of 16) - Brazil eliminated by Norway
2. **United States 1-4 Belgium** (Round of 16) - Host nation eliminated
3. **England 1-2 Argentina** (Semifinal) - England's quest for first World Cup since 1966 ended
4. **Norway 1-0 England** (Quarterfinal) - England eliminated in extra time

### Countries Making Their World Cup Debut
- **Curaçao**: First FIFA World Cup appearance
- **Cape Verde**: First FIFA World Cup appearance
